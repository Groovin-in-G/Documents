Hardware Sampler → Analog Mixer
Simplest setup. Sampler stereo outputs go straight into a stereo channel on the mixer. Mixer main outs go to monitors.
Ableton Aux Tracks → Audio Interface → Mixer
Ableton sends individual aux/stem tracks out via the audio interface's multiple outputs. Each output pair feeds a channel on the analog mixer — giving you hands-on control of the DAW mix.
Ableton + Interface + Hardware Sampler → Patchbay → Mixer
Everything routes through a patchbay first. The patchbay normalises connections so you can repatch on the fly without reaching behind gear. Interface outputs and sampler outputs all land on the patchbay — the patchbay's outputs feed the mixer channels.
